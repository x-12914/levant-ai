# PRODUCT.md — Levant AI

> Context for the `impeccable` design skill. Register: **product** (the design serves the tool, it is not the product).

## Product purpose

Levant AI is a web trading workspace that aggregates a trader's accounts across multiple
brokers (forex, stocks, crypto) into one read-only command center. Version 1 reads balances,
positions and trade history; it analyzes, scores and explains. It does not place orders.

On top of aggregation it layers: an AI assistant for pre-trade questions, analyst-insight
summaries, a strategy lab (pair-trading correlation, no-code builders, backtests) and a
traders community (social + copy-trading shell).

## Users

- **Active retail + semi-pro traders** running several broker accounts (e.g. FXPro/MT5,
  Binance, Interactive Brokers) who currently tab between platforms and spreadsheets.
- They watch P/L during market hours, often on a large monitor in a dim room, and need
  red/green outcome signal at a glance plus the ability to drill into a single leg of a
  pair trade.
- Mixed expertise: novices want the AI to explain; pros want dense tables and fast filters.

## The scene (theme justification)

A trader scanning aggregated P/L across brokers on a 27-inch monitor during a session,
room lights low, eyes on the tape for hours. Dark surface reduces glare and lets the
green/red semantic colors do the talking. Dark is forced by the scene, not by category reflex.

## Brand & tone

- Name: **Levant AI**. Voice: precise, calm, never hyped. It states numbers and risk plainly.
- Confident terminal-grade tooling, not a flashy crypto-bro app and not a stuffy bank.
- Copy is terse and literal. No exclamation marks, no "supercharge your trades."

## Anti-references (what to avoid)

- Navy-and-gold "premium finance" cliché.
- Neon-on-black crypto casino look.
- Generic SaaS-cream dashboards with identical icon-heading-text card grids.
- The hero-metric template (one giant gradient number with supporting stats).

## Strategic principles

1. **Read-only is a feature, not a limitation.** Surfaces should make clear nothing here
   moves money; the value is clarity and analysis.
2. **Numbers are the interface.** Tabular figures, right-aligned, monospaced digits,
   semantic color. Chrome recedes; data leads.
3. **One glance, then drill.** Top-level state readable in a second; detail one click away.
4. **Trust through transparency.** Show source of every trade (EA, pair leg, manual),
   show fees and execution, never hide a failure (surface partial failures loudly).
